Design System at Dow Jones

Overview

Dow Jones required a unified design system to streamline multiple B2B and B2C brands, ensuring scalability, efficiency, and consistency across all platforms. By implementing a token-first strategy, we enabled teams to reuse components, customize branding efficiently, and reduce duplication.

The Problem

Separate design approaches across brands led to inconsistencies, duplicated efforts, and increased costs. The lack of a centralized system hindered scalability, slowed down development, and created friction between design and engineering teams.

Hypothesis

By bridging design and development through a strategic design system powered by Design Tokens, we could streamline design processes, enhance cross-brand consistency, and optimize workflows. The integration of AI-driven documentation and data analysis would further improve decision-making and accelerate design-to-development workflows. This approach aimed to reduce complexity, improve efficiency, and foster better collaboration across teams.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Strategic Development: Co-led the creation and management of a unified design system, ensuring alignment across B2B and B2C products.
  • Bridging Design & Development: Translated business, technical, and design needs to facilitate seamless collaboration across multidisciplinary teams.
  • AI-Powered Optimization: Leveraged AI to enhance documentation, analyze data, and improve efficiency in design and development workflows.
  • Team Leadership & Mentorship: Mentored and led designers, developers, and contractors, fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning.
  • System Scalability & Efficiency: Reorganized repositories and Storybook, refining the component library to reduce complexity by 20% and improve cross-brand consistency.
  • Improved Communication & Productivity: Strengthened team coordination between design, development, and accessibility teams, leading to a 30% productivity boost.

Technologies

Figma - Git – React – SAFe - Design Tokens. – Jira – ChatGPT – Copilot – Vertex AI – Confluence

Output

  • Optimized Component Library: Reduced library size by 20%, enhancing reusability and maintainability.
  • Increased Efficiency: Streamlined design and development processes, accelerating project timelines and reducing redundancies.
  • Enhanced Brand Consistency: Achieved stronger visual coherence across all brands through standardized components and design tokens.
  • AI-Driven Innovation: Improved decision-making and workflow efficiency through AI-powered documentation and automation.
  • 30% Productivity Boost: Strengthened cross-functional collaboration, reducing friction and improving output across teams.
