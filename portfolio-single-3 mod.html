Project Description

Far far away, behind the word mountains, far from the countries Vokalia and Consonantia, there live the blind texts. Separated they live in Bookmarksgrove right at the coast of the Semantics, a large language ocean.

Project Date March 9th, 2020
Role Identity, Web Design
Client unslate
Visit https://unslate.co/

Overview

Dow Jones needed a unified design system to streamline multiple B2B and B2C brands, allowing teams to reuse components and customize branding efficiently.

The Problem

Separate design approaches across brands caused inconsistencies and duplicated efforts, leading to inefficiency and higher costs.

Hypothesis

By adopting a token-first strategy and creating a unified design system, we could streamline design processes, reduce duplication, and ensure consistency across all brands. This would allow teams to reuse components and customize branding with minimal effort, leading to increased efficiency, improved collaboration, and faster delivery times.
